United Nations High Commissioner for Refugees (UNHCR) – Access to Education for Refugees – June 27, 2020

  • by livemunc
  • May 24, 2020July 5, 2020

What if you could create policies that would actually be discussed in a UN body? What if you were real delegates to actual countries in the UNHCR?

Today, LiveMUN has collaborated with UNCHR for a once in a lifetime opportunity for your resolutions to be heard and discussed worldwide!

The UNHCR has challenged you to come up with resolutions for one of their real issues which is providing access to education for refugees.

LiveMUN will be hosting the UNHCR committee where you will have a chance to come up with a resolution that we will submit to the UNHCR. The UNHCR will in turn look over your resolutions, give the best ideas awards, and share them with policymakers.

This is an opportunity to harness all that you’ve learned to make a significant global difference and help people all over the world from quarantine behind your screens.

We are sure that you, the LiveMUN Family, are up to the challenge!
